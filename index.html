What are Trade Unions?

Introduction

Trade unions are organizations formed by workers from related fields that work for the common interest of their members. They help protect and improve wages, working conditions, and benefits.

Functions of Trade Unions

Trade unions work to improve the economic and social conditions of their members. Their main roles include negotiating with employers, providing legal support, and ensuring workplace safety.

Examples of Trade Unions

  • American Federation of Labor (AFL-CIO)
  • United Auto Workers (UAW)
  • International Brotherhood of Teamsters (IBT)
